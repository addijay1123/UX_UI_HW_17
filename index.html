About Me

Hi there, I’m Addi Jones, a new UX designer. I am highly motivated in my new career path and eager to put my talents to use! My experience in public health research has fostered skills in clear communication, critical thinking, and empathy. This experience along with a unique and creative outlook, lends to a design technique that is collaborative, playful, and memorable for users. I aim to take on projects that are centered around making people’s lives easier and access more equitable for everyone.
